About the company

Khojo Right Now is a dynamic and innovative Offline-to-Online (O2O) platform that redefines the landscape of smart networking, registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs and proudly associated with Startup India. Our commitment to innovation and inclusivity led us to collaborate with the Ministry of Textiles in Rajasthan. Together, we empowered artisans by creating personalized profiles, garnering heartfelt appreciation in the form of a commendation letter for our dedication to uplifting grassroots talents.

Our platform serves as a bridge between the digital and physical worlds, offering users a seamless and efficient way to connect with individuals and businesses in their immediate vicinity. Within moments, you can have a stunning, fully-functional one-page website that boasts an array of smart features tailored to uplift your professional profile or business stature.
